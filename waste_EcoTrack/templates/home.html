ABOUT US

The Waste EcoTrack Initiative aims to revolutionize waste management in Kisumu by leveraging cutting-edge technologies for greater transparency, efficiency, and community engagement. Our platform empowers residents to actively participate in sustainable waste management practices, transforming Kisumu into a cleaner, greener city.

We envision a cleaner and more sustainable Kisumu, where technology empowers communities to actively participate in waste management. By fostering transparency, accountability, and community engagement, the Waste EcoTrack Initiative is committed to creating a healthier environment for current and future generations.
